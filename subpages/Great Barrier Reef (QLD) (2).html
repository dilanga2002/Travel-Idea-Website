History

Formation over Millions of Years

The Great Barrier Reef, off Queensland's coast, formed over millions of years through intricate coral growth. This natural wonder, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, represents an ecosystem of unparalleled biodiversity. Its history is a testament to the slow and steady process of coral development, creating a breathtaking marine environment that attracts visitors worldwide.
